Prompt tạo trang web trong Figma

Design a modern landing page website for a beauty service platform called “XINH.DAY”.

The platform connects users with beauty services such as dress rental, makeup artists, photographers, and accessories rental.

Target Users

Women aged 16–35 who need beauty services for events such as:

Graduation

Birthday

Parties

Photoshoots

Special events

They value convenience, affordable price, and stylish appearance.

Website Structure
1. Hero Section

Large banner with elegant fashion / beauty visuals

Headline:
“Your All-in-One Beauty Service Platform”

Subtext:
“Find, compare and book dresses, makeup artists, and photographers in one place.”

CTA buttons:

Browse Services

Book Now

2. Services Section

Display service categories in cards with icons/images:

Dress Rental

Accessories Rental

Makeup & Hair

Photographer Booking

Full Beauty Combo

Each card contains:

Image

Title

Short description

“View Details” button

3. How It Works

3-step process with icons:

Search services by event, style, or budget

Compare prices and reviews

Book online and receive confirmation

4. Featured Concepts / Combos

Show packages such as:

Graduation Combo

Birthday Party Look

Elegant Photoshoot Style

Each package includes:

Dress

Makeup

Photographer

Accessories

Display with price and “Book Now” button.

5. Platform Features

Highlight platform benefits:

Online booking system

Transparent pricing

Chatbot consultation

Review and rating system

Booking management

Reminder for return time

Use icons and short descriptions.

6. Customer Reviews

Show testimonials from users with:

User avatar

Rating stars

Short review

7. Footer

Include:

About XINH.DAY

Contact

Social media links

Customer support

Terms & policy

Design Style

Modern beauty / fashion aesthetic

Soft pastel colors (pink, beige, white)

Elegant typography

Rounded cards and soft shadows

Mobile-friendly layout

Clean and minimal UI
